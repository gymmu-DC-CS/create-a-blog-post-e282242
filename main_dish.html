Main Dish

Lasagna

Ingrediens

  • 800g ground beef
  • 1 can of kidney beans (800g)
  • 1 can of kidney beans (800g)
  • 1 can of corn kernels (250g)
  • 3 large onions
  • 500g pureed tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • cayenne pepper
  • sweet paprika
  • a pinch of salt

Instruction

  1. Fry the ground beef and diced onions in hot olive oil for a few minutes.
  2. Add the rest (beans and corn with liquid!)
  3. Season to taste and simmer for 30 minutes.

    4.  If necessary, add garlic or savory or caraway or chili beans. As an alternative to the canned liquid, you can add vegetable broth.
