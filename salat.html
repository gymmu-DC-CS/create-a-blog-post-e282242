Salad

Potato salad

Ingrediens

  • 700g Potatos
  • 1.2l water
  • 5 pinches of salt
  • pepper
  • 25ml vinegar
  • 20ml oil
  • 4 onions
  • 2 tablespoons fondor
  • 250ml hot water

Instruction

  1. Boil the potatoes for about 30 minutes
  2. Then check whether they are cooked, let them cool slightly and peel them.
  3. Cut cold potatoes into slices.
  4. Make a marinade from oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, onions and fondant.
  5. Dilute with 1/4 liter of hot water and fold loosely into the potatoes.

Don't put the salate in the fridge when you are finished.
